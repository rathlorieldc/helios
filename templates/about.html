About


Helios uses modeled history and projection data to help you choose the best possible place for retirement while considering climate change factors. The projections are based on two scenarios: humans changing course to significantly lower emissions, or humans continuing on the current course to increasingly higher emissions.

To use the program, choose a location and the decade you plan to retire and we will search our database for climate change projections so you can make an informed decision about where to retire.



All data is courtesy of the NOAA Climate Explorer.
